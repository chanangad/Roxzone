Roxzone Support

Support and launch notes.

How purchase works

The watch app generates a claim code. Checkout binds your payment and email to that code. Use Razorpay for India payments or PayPal for international checkout. After payment, the watch refreshes its license state from the backend.

Reinstall or new watch

Open Restore Purchase from the watch, enter the same email used during checkout, and we rebind the paid license to the current claim code.

What to customize before launch

  • Replace placeholder worker and GitHub URLs.
  • Add your support email and refund text.
  • Configure both PayPal and Razorpay webhook URLs to this Worker.
  • Update the Connect IQ listing with external-payment disclosure.
